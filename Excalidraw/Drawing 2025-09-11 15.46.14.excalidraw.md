WAVE THEORY ^eYFygRKA

Wave theory is based on Huygens' wave principle:
* Every point on a wavefront acts as a source of secondary wavelets.
* These wavelets spread forward with the same wave speed.
* The new wavefront at a later time is the surface tangent to all secondary wavelets.
* This principle allows prediction of how waves propagate. ^9eBuw7CO
